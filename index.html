About Us

Porky Prints is a Transition Year mini company, ran by Ruaidhri McCool, Luka Parkes, and Patrick Redmond.

We have some premade posters, or you can commission us to create anything you want!

For teachers, that could be a poster explaining, for example, Pascal's Triangle. For students, we could create a poster about an interest of yours, such as a game!

A3 Premade Designs: €3 base fee PLUS €2 per page printed.


A4 Premade Designs: €3 base fee PLUS €1.50 per page printed.
