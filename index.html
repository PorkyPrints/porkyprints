About Us

Porky Prints is a Transition Year mini company, ran by Luka Parkes, Ruaidhri McCool and Patrick Redmond. We have some premade posters, or you can commission us to create anything you want! For teachers, that could be a poster explaining, for example, Pascal's Triangle. For students, we could create a poster about an interest of yours!
